**⚗️ Combined-Color R-Cane Reaction Table**

**Mechanics:**

- Roll **1d6** when you mix **two colors**.
- If mixing **three or more**, roll **twice** and combine the results (or escalate effects).
- Effects last **1 hour**, unless stated otherwise.
- Overdose checks occur at **DC 15 Constitution** due to arcane instability.

---

**💜 PURPLE + BLUE — _“The Oracle’s Circuit”_**

1. Future-sight overload: gain +2 to all Intelligence checks, but disadvantage on Wisdom saves.
2. You see _every possible outcome_ of each action for 10 minutes — incapacitating unless someone grounds you.
3. You can ask 1 yes/no question to the DM as if casting _augury_.
4. You speak in unstoppable, correct predictions — allies gain +1 to attack rolls for 10 minutes.
5. Your brain splits into two voices arguing logic vs prophecy; concentration checks are made with advantage.
6. A spectral, data-like apparition of yourself appears, giving cryptic advice.

**💥 MIXING THREE OR MORE COLORS**

For chaotic multi-color blends, choose **TWO effects** from above and **add one of these global surges**:

- **Prismatic Pulse:** 10 ft radius, 1d6 random elemental damage to all creatures (Dex save halves).
- **Arcane Whiplash:** All spellcasters within 30 ft feel a magical shock; they must make concentration saves.
- **Temporal Blink:** User teleports up to 30 ft in a random direction.
- **Reality Ripple:** A minor magical anomaly persists in the area for 1 hour.
- **Personality Overwrite:** For 10 minutes, the user acts like one of their past selves — or someone they’ve met.

**Memory Bloom:** User regains a vivid, emotional memory that can inspire roleplay or lead to plot.

**⚠️ COMMON CROSS-COLOR MUTATIONS**

Occur if a character uses **two or more colors** frequently.

**Arcane Vitiligo**

Skin forms glowing prismatic patterns.

**Temporal Flicker**

You freeze for 1 second at random.

**Resonance Pulse**

When emotionally overwhelmed, you release a ripple of force (1d4).

**Casting Echo**

Your spells create ghostly after-images.

**Elemental Drip**

Your sweat, tears, or blood take on elemental properties.

---

**🧬 SEVERE MUTATION EVENTS**

Trigger after major overdose or choice-based progression.

**Arcane Organ Failure**

One organ becomes partly magical:

- A crystalline liver
- A lightning-charged heart
- Blood replaced with glowing fluid

These offer boons AND curses.

**Memory Scar**

A memory becomes permanently enhanced… or erased.

**Planar Resonance**

Your body starts phasing into another plane at night.

**Unstable Magic Core**

Spells cast within 10 ft of you have a 5% wild surge chance.
