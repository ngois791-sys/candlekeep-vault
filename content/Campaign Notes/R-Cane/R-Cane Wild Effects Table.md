**🎲 R-Cane Wild Effects Table (1d100 Roll)**

_100 unique arcane surges — flavorful, chaotic, campaign-enriching._

---

**1–10: Minor Visual and Sensory Surges**

1. Eyes glow with neon static for 1 hour; darkvision +10 ft.
2. You leave faint, colorful footprints that fade after 10 minutes.
3. Your voice echoes with a delayed second voice. No mechanical effect.
4. All food tastes like your favorite meal for 1 hour.
5. Colors become hyper-saturated; Perception checks involving sight gain +1.
6. Glimmering arcane motes orbit your head like fireflies.
7. Your hair and clothing flow as if underwater.
8. You smell ozone constantly; immune to nausea for 1 hour.
9. When you blink, you leave brief after-images.
10. Your pupils turn into arcane sigils for 1 hour.

---

**11–20: Physical Twitches and Weird Perks**

11. Hands shake with power; unarmed strikes deal +1 damage for 10 minutes.
12. Feet spark with micro-lightning; advantage on balance checks.
13. You can snap your fingers to create harmless sparks.
14. Your heartbeat can be heard within 5 ft. Slightly unsettling.
15. You feel weightless; jumping distance +5 ft.
16. Blood glows faintly through the veins.
17. Your skin hums like quiet chimes.
18. Your shadow moves half a second behind you.
19. Your body temperature rises; resistant to cold for 10 minutes.
20. Every time you speak, faint smoke curls from your mouth.

---

**21–30: Combat Flickers**

21. Next attack that hits deals an extra 1d6 force damage.
22. Next attack that misses ricochets; reroll once with -2 penalty.
23. Your weapon crackles with unstable energy; advantage on Intimidation checks.
24. Your muscles surge—gain +2 to Strength checks for 10 minutes.
25. Your footsteps emit tiny thunder pops; enemies within 5 ft have -1 to Stealth.
26. The next spell you cast glows violently; add +1 to its save DC.
27. You briefly phase; gain resistance to nonmagical bludgeoning for 1 minute.
28. Your skin flickers with elemental colors; gain +1 AC for 1 minute.
29. A weird ripple rewinds time for a blink; you may reroll initiative once.
30. Sparks lash out; every creature within 5 ft takes 1 lightning damage.

---

**31–40: Social & Emotional Distortions**

31. Your charisma surges—gain +2 to Persuasion for 10 minutes.
32. You become _too_ honest; disadvantage on Deception checks.
33. You can mimic any voice you’ve heard in the last 24 hours.
34. You become intensely sentimental; advantage on Insight checks.
35. Your mood becomes contagious; allies gain +1 on morale-based checks.
36. You smell like a fresh storm; NPCs react with curiosity or caution.
37. You refuse to whisper; all your speech is 20% louder.
38. You become hyper-affectionate; advantage on calming NPCs.
39. You speak in dramatic flourishes and metaphors.
40. You temporarily forget social boundaries; could be awkward.

**41–50: Moderate Arcane Surges**

41. Casting a spell causes your body to emit a shockwave (no damage).
42. You can see magical auras faintly for 10 minutes.
43. Small illusions (sparks, symbols, animals) appear around you randomly.
44. You are surrounded by a faint magical shield; gain 5 temporary HP.
45. Your footsteps create ghostly echoes; +1 to stealth _or_ disadvantage (roll d2).
46. A random object in your possession levitates for 1 minute.
47. Your hair grows 3 inches instantly. Color may shift.
48. You can speak telepathically to creatures within 10 ft for 5 minutes.
49. You float 1 inch off the ground—cosmetic only.
50. You briefly surge with confidence; automatic success on the next ability check of your choice.

**51–60: Chaotic Body Mutations (Temporary)**

51. Your skin becomes glassy and reflective for 10 minutes.
52. Your eyes take on an animal form (cat, serpent, goat). Gain advantage on Perception.
53. Your fingers elongate slightly; +2 to Sleight of Hand for 10 minutes.
54. Your teeth glow; unsettling, but no effect.
55. Muscles ripple with energy; gain +1 to Melee attack rolls for 10 minutes.
56. You grow tiny harmless horns.
57. Your voice jumps octaves uncontrollably.
58. Your bones vibrate audibly when you walk.
59. The ends of your hair spark with lightning.
60. Your ears become elongated temporarily; +1 to hearing-based checks.

**61–70: Unpredictable Environmental Effects**

61. Nearby candles extinguish or ignite randomly.
62. Metal objects within 5 ft rattle.
63. Water ripples when you approach.
64. Dust and sand form swirling patterns around you.
65. Animals in the area behave strangely (approach curiously or flee).
66. The weather shifts slightly—clouds part or darken above you.
67. Shadows lengthen unnaturally for 30 seconds.
68. Plants grow an inch when you walk past.
69. Static shocks affect any creature who touches you.
70. Random spectral whispers reverberate around you for 1 minute.

**71–80: Strong, Risky Magical Surges**

71. You can cast _Thaumaturgy_ at will for 10 minutes.
72. One random item in your pocket teleports 10 ft away.
73. A harmless familiar-like spirit appears and follows you for 1 hour.
74. A random spell you know casts itself at a random target (DM chooses fairly).
75. You gain the ability to see invisible creatures for 1 minute.
76. Your next spell has a wild surge (+1d4 bonus effect).
77. Uncontrolled levitation (5 ft); lasts 1 minute.
78. A mirror-image version of you appears and mocks you (no combat stats).
79. You become intangible for 3 seconds—long enough to fall through floors.

**81–90: Dangerous or Dramatic Effects**

81. Your body erupts with arcane backlash; take 2d6 force damage.
82. You involuntarily cast _Gust of Wind_.
83. Your pulse becomes a beacon; creatures can sense you within 60 ft.
84. You accidentally teleport 10d10 feet in a random direction.

88. The ground beneath you cracks, emitting harmless magical light.
89. You become unable to speak for 1 minute—but can cast spells silently.
90. You briefly enter a semi-ethereal state; you cannot be hit until your next turn but cannot interact either.

**91–100: Cataclysmic / Story-Altering Surges**

91. You glimpse a vision of a possible future — DM gives one cryptic clue.
92. Your soul partially detaches; you’re invisible for 30 seconds.
93. All metal within 10 ft magnetically slams toward you (Dex save or 1d6 damage).
94. You temporarily swap bodies with a nearby creature (1 minute).
95. A random spell effect from a random class triggers (DM chooses fun option).
96. A planar entity notices you — could be good or very bad.
97. You open a 5 ft crack into another plane. Something _might_ come through.
98. You gain a permanent minor mutation (DM decides).
99. Time loops for 6 seconds; your last turn repeats exactly.
100. You explode with raw magic — take 4d10 damage, **but** gain a permanent +1 to an ability score of the DM’s choice (or tied to the color of the R-Cane used).
