# Lucifurry — Bhaal Arc Tracker

_Scion of the Three (Bhaal) | Level 3 Rogue_

---

## Quick Character Reference

- **Real name:** Lucifurry (Luci)
- **Background:** Orphan, bounced foster homes until 18
- **Rogue origin:** Stole food/clothes to survive — taken in by a band of rogues
- **Sister:** A tiefling, publicly executed in the street
- **Happiest memory:** Stealing together with his sister in the orphanage
- **Deepest fear:** Failure and dishonouring his sister's memory
- **Motivation:** Avenge his sister. Live the life she would've wanted for them both
- **Secret:** Spent significant time in organized crime — escaped and fled the country
- **God:** Bhaal (chosen at level 3) — Resistance: Poison | Cantrip: Blade Ward

---

## The Bhaal Hooks — Chalet Brantifax Arc

> [!important] DM Philosophy Bhaal does not announce himself. He rewards, he watches, he whispers. None of these hooks should feel like a divine encounter — they should feel like something is _wrong_ with Luci, and only he notices. Keep everything subtle. Let the player connect the dots.

---

### Hook 1 — The Graveyard (Area C9)

**Location:** Chalet Brantifax graveyard, Baron Brantifax's headstone **When to trigger:** The first time Luci steps near or onto Baron Brantifax's grave

- [x] **Triggered**

> [!note] Adventure Text The adventure states that any character who stands on Baron Brantifax's grave "feels a harmless chill run through them."

> [!warning] DM Note — Run It Like This Describe the chill to Morvax and Vyr'ashen normally. Then turn to Luci's player privately (or pause and say "Luci, you feel something different"). He feels **warmth** instead — not heat, just warmth. Like the moment you step into a firelit room after a cold walk. And the unmistakable feeling of being **watched and approved of**. No voice. No figure. Just that. It stops the moment he steps off the grave.

---

### Hook 2 — The Haunted Well (Area C3)

**Location:** Storage cellar, the haunted well **When to trigger:** When Luci passes within earshot of the well

- [x] **Triggered**

> [!note] Adventure Text The well whispers _"Brorn! Where are you, boy?"_ — the ghost of the baron's lost mastiff, calling for its master.

> [!warning] DM Note — Run It Like This Every party member hears the dog whisper. Then, underneath it — quieter, layered beneath — Luci alone hears a second voice. A girl. Just his name. Once. Then silence. It is unmistakably his sister's voice. The moment he tries to focus on it or move toward the sound, it's gone. Do not confirm or deny what he heard if he asks the party. Nobody else caught it.

---

### Hook 3 — The Orcus Figurine (Area C14)

**Location:** Baron's Loft, hidden in rubble — currently in Vinique's possession **When to trigger:** When Luci comes within 5 feet of the figurine

- [ ] **Triggered**

> [!note] Adventure Text The Orcus Figurine smells of decaying flesh. This scent is detectable within 5 feet. The figurine is a desecrated evil relic of the Demon Prince of Undeath.

> [!warning] DM Note — Run It Like This Describe the smell clearly to the party — rotting, sweet-sick, wrong. Watch for reactions. Do **not** prompt Luci's player. If his player doesn't volunteer a reaction, quietly ask: _"What does Luci notice about the smell?"_ The answer is that he doesn't react. It doesn't register as unpleasant. If pressed, he has to actually think about it and realise with quiet horror that it smells almost... familiar. Like something from a dream he can't quite place. Do not over-explain. Drop it.

---

### Hook 4 — The Attic Nursery (Area C15)

**Location:** Third floor attic nursery — Sylphene's poltergeist **When to trigger:** When Luci enters the nursery

- [x] **Triggered**

> [!note] Adventure Text Sylphene's spirit manifests as a poltergeist that tries to frighten away intruders — rattling, shrieking, making the room feel hostile.

> [!warning] DM Note — Run It Like This When Morvax and Vyr'ashen enter, run the poltergeist as normal — unsettling, aggressive, frightening. When Luci enters or is already inside, the poltergeist **goes completely still**. No rattling. No shrieking. It simply stares in his direction. Then, once — softly, in the voice of a small girl — the room makes the sound of a child laughing. A specific laugh. Luci recognizes it. Bhaal reaching into his grief and pulling one thread. The poltergeist does not attack Luci.

---

### Hook 5 — First Bloodthirst Trigger (Shadowfell Necropolis)

**Location:** The Shadowfell — necropolis outside the Harrn Mausoleum **When to trigger:** The very first time Luci's Bloodthirst feature activates in combat

- [ ] **Triggered**

> [!note] How Bloodthirst Works When an enemy Luci can see within 30 ft takes damage and becomes Bloodied (drops to half HP) but isn't killed, he can use his Reaction to teleport within 5 ft of that enemy and make a melee attack.

> [!warning] DM Note — Run It Like This When a ghoul hits the Bloodied threshold, before Luci's player decides to use the reaction, describe it happening first: _"Luci — your body is already moving before your mind catches up. You're across the battlefield, blade in hand, standing over the ghoul before you registered the choice."_ Then let the player make the attack roll. Afterward, quietly ask: _"Is Luci disturbed by that... or did it feel right?"_ Let that question sit unanswered. Don't rush past it.

---

### Hook 6 — Drovath Harrn's Last Words (Shadowfell Necropolis)

**Location:** Harrn Mausoleum — after defeating the wight Drovath Harrn **When to trigger:** The moment Drovath Harrn is reduced to 0 HP

- [ ] **Triggered**

> [!note] Adventure Text Drovath Harrn is a wight who inhabits the mausoleum atop which the gargoyles perch. He wears a ring of jumping on his right hand.

> [!warning] DM Note — Run It Like This As Drovath crumbles, describe the death normally to the party. Then, privately to Luci's player only: beneath the sound of combat and crumbling bone, he alone catches something in the death rattle — a whisper meant only for him. Deliver this line quietly, directly to the player: _"He sees you, child. He has always seen you."_ Nobody else hears it. If Luci tells the party, nobody can confirm it happened.

---

### Hook 7 — The Long Game (Ongoing / Future Sessions)

**Location:** No fixed location — slow burn revelation across the campaign **When to trigger:** When Luci begins seriously pursuing his sister's killers OR when the organized crime past resurfaces

- [ ] **Triggered**

> [!warning] DM Note — The Full Picture Luci's organized crime organization had **Bhaal cult ties he didn't know about**. The people who ordered his sister's execution had dealings with that same organization. Bhaal did not choose Luci randomly — the murder of his sister was the act that first drew Bhaal's eye. Not because Luci committed it, but because the grief and hunger for vengeance that followed was exactly what Bhaal feeds on. The god has been patient. He has been watching since that day in the street. When the time is right, a cult contact — someone from Luci's criminal past — can resurface and begin to connect these threads for him. Or against him.

## DM Notes — Things to Remember

- Bhaal **never announces himself**. Everything is deniable
- Keep all hooks private to Luci's player where possible
- The other party members — **Morvax** and **Vyr'ashen** — should notice something is slightly off about Luci over time, but never be able to pin it down
- Luci's player chose Bhaal willingly. Trust him to lean into it — give him the rope
- His sister's name was never given in the questionnaire — consider asking the player if he has one in mind. It will make Hook 2 and Hook 4 hit much harder
