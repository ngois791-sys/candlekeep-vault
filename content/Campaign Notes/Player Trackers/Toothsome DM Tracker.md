## Tempus & The Steel Fang — DM Context 
Tempus is the greater god of war — chaotic neutral, no fixed allegiances. His priests serve as wartime chaplains on any side, uphold the rules of honourable combat, and preserve the names of the fallen. They do not preach. They fight. 

The **Order of the Steel Fang** was reformed in 1467 DR following the Tempuswrath — an event where Tempus physically manifested on a battlefield and slew nobles who had hired Steel Fangs against each other. The order now operates exclusively as an internal church force: disciplinary enforcement, relic escort, and hunters of heresy. Members travel alone or in small units and answer directly to senior church leadership. 
> [!important] DM Note — Steel Fang Hook 
> > An adventuring Steel Fang is either on a mission or has been cut loose for a reason. Which is it for Toothsome? Fill this in once the player confirms. 

> [!note] Tone 
> > Tempus priests are not paladins. No preaching, no recruiting. A Steel Fang member should feel like a special operator — quiet, precise, carrying a complex history. See [[Tempus DM Reference]] for full priestly ranks, rituals, and hooks.
