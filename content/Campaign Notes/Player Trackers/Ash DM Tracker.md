## Backstory Summary

Ash came from a simple family of tradespeople — a blacksmith father and a mother who ran the storefront. At 8 years old, he watched a corrupt sheriff attempt to extort his family's store and kill them when they refused to comply. He spent the next 8 years learning to control and weaponize his magic. At 16 he killed the sheriff, and then needed to flee and earn money. Sorcery was the fastest way to make a living for anyone who wasn't a nobleman.

> [!important] DM Secret Ash cannot use magic during a full moon. He has not disclosed this to the party. This is a significant vulnerability — track the lunar calendar and consider when this will matter most.

> [!warning] Enemies from the Past Law enforcement in his home town may remember him. He killed a sheriff and fled. No friends from his past — he left everything behind.
