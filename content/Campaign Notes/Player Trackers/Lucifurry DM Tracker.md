## Backstory Summary

Lucifurry is a tiefling orphan who bounced between foster homes until the age of 18. He and his little sister were close — they would steal from other kids in the orphanage and sell their belongings together. His world broke when his sister was publicly executed in the street for being a tiefling. After that, Luci turned to crime, spending significant time in organized crime before escaping and fleeing the country. A band of rogues took him in when he was young, cementing his profession. He still maintains his aptitude for crime.

> [!important] DM Secret Luci's organized crime organization had Bhaal cult ties he didn't know about. The people who ordered his sister's execution had dealings with that same organization. Bhaal did not choose Luci randomly — the murder of his sister was the act that first drew Bhaal's eye. He has been watching since that day in the street.
