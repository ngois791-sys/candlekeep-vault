## Backstory Summary

Morvax is an elf with no family — all dead, and he never knew them. He is entirely self-taught, learning magic secretly through books he could find or steal, and through forgotten relics he came across. His happiest memory is casting his first small fireball. His worst: that same recklessness eventually led him to accidentally burn his entire village to the ground. He was kicked out as a result. He became an adventurer because he needed money and had nothing better to do.

> [!important] DM Secret The arcane symbols Morvax studied were never truly forgotten relics. He is the only person alive who can still hear them whispering back to him. He has never told anyone what they ask of him. This is a major unresolved thread — what are they asking?

## Character Arc / Story Goals

- **Ultimate goal:** To be as powerful as possible
- **Driving fear:** Losing control of his power — a fear grounded in real consequence (the village)
- **Inner conflict:** A man who craves power but knows firsthand what happens when it slips

> [!note] DM Hook Idea The whispering relics are the thread to pull. What do they want? Who put them there? Does Morvax's power come from something he doesn't fully understand yet?
