# 2-1-2026 Campaign Session Notes

## Last Session Recap

- The last session was full of surprises for me. You accepted another bounty from the avowed after learning that books were coming to life and killed someone. After some investigating, you discovered that all the books were bought in Baulder's Gate from a shop in the The Wide. After some scouting, and Luci buying some R-Cane (red, purple, green) you three discovered that the shop the books were bought from was called Amberdune Books. You spoke to the workers and got a weird vibe from them so you decided to watch the stall for a bit and watched them do a shift change. Luci decided to follow them while the others stayed behind. Luci followed them all the way back to their house where he snuck in with distractions from some children tieflings, and stole some gold, some books, oh and the heart of a Sphinx. You guys got back to an inn and Luci had a vision where the Sphinx (Nidalia) told him to return the heart to the jackelweres. You three went back to the house, and after a little bit of fighting and running you ended up talking to the leader (Korvala) and came to an agreement. You decided to pay back the gold, and then some and help them with the ritual to bring Nidalia the Sphinx of knowledge. And that is where we left off.
