# 11-22-25 Campaign Session Notes

## Last Session Recap

- To start this campaign, we started in a nice little tavern called The Battleaxe off the Green Fields Road, where our three adventurers (Ash, Luci, and Morvax) were resting and drinking, looking for their next big score. There Ash made some new friends at the bar, Morvax gambled with money, and Luci gambled with his life trying to flirt with Gorga. After a bit you were directed to the bulletin board after two individuals dressed in purple robes seemed to just magically appear in the tavern. They put up a sign and then went out the back door where Ash followed them and witnessed them vanish through a portal of light. Morvax checked out the sign and saw an opportunity so make some easy money finding a monk in the library of Candlekeep, the worlds largest bank of knowledge.  
- As the three of you arrived at the gate of Candlekeep you realized you didn't have the proper requirements to get in. Morvax quickly looked into his bag and found a book to trade entire into but sadly they already had the book. Ash then pulled out the sign from the bulletin board, stating you were here to help find a missing person and that you need to see Brother Alec. They let you into the Keep and led you to the Temple of Oghma where Brother Alec was supposed to be. Once inside you saw 4 people sitting there, Luci decided to man-handle the old man (Brother Alec), to which the three of you noticed the 4 gargoyles turn and stare at Luci. 
- Once you left the Temple Brother Alec led you to the Library and to Materous's room, informing you of his disappearance. After some investigation checks you discovered the secret word to open a portal of which the 4 of you entering finding Materous. He thanked you for opening the portal and asked the 4 of you to continue to look around while he went back to his room to study the imp statue he was holding, saying he would keep the portal open on the other end. But once he passed the portal you all heard a loud scream and the portal closed.
- Your only option was to explore Fistandia's Mansion and find a way out. Along the way you discovered a couple things about Fistandia. She gets her power from a god (Mistra) and made a deal with her to get this magical mansion. She hasn't appeared in the mansion in a long time and you found her homunculus Cumin and Freyots Homunculus Coriander. After some run ins with many magic items including a quasit, animated swords, a bookcase, and books, you finally discovered the magic word, liberty and escaped the mansion with cumin and coriander only to find Materous dead and immediately attacked by an imp. You defeated the imp and received your reward.
