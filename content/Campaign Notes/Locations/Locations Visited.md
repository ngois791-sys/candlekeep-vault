# Locations Visited
*A record of every city, town, and notable place the party has visited. Individual shops, NPCs, and sub-locations have their own files — linked below each entry.*

---

## The Battleaxe Tavern
**Type:** Roadside Tavern | **Road:** The Green Fields Road

A well-worn tavern sitting along the Green Fields Road — the kind of place adventurers end up when they have nowhere better to be. Warm, loud, and unpretentious.

**Party History:**
- The campaign's starting point. Ash, Luci, and Morvax were drinking here when two purple-robed figures appeared inside the tavern, pinned a notice to the bulletin board, and vanished through a portal of light out the back. Ash followed them and witnessed it. Morvax read the notice. That was enough.
- Luci tried to flirt with Gorga Stonehand, the orc barkeeper, and failed spectacularly.

**Notable Locations:**
- [[Gorga Stonehand]] — barkeeper, fights with a battleaxe named Bloodsong

## Candlekeep
**Type:** Fortress Library | **Region:** The Sword Coast, southeast of Baldur's Gate

The world's largest repository of knowledge — a great library fortress built on a rocky crag overlooking the Sea of Swords. Entry requires donating a book or scroll of sufficient rarity and value. The Avowed, a scholarly monastic order, maintain and protect it. Candlekeep is the party's home base for the campaign.

**Party History:**
- Arrived in Session 1 using the bulletin board notice as their entry offering.
- Met Brother Alec at the Temple of Oghma — Luci grabbed him, the gargoyles stared.
- Completed the bounty for the missing sage Matreous.
- Completed a second bounty investigating animated books.
- Ongoing base of operations between adventures.

**Notable Locations Within:**
- [[The Hearth]] — the Candlekeep tavern. Run by Serelyth Willowhush.
- [[Erudite Outfitters & Clothiers]] — armor and clothing. Run by Feldmar Bisset III.
- [[Smithy and Stables]] — weapons, repairs, and mounts. Run by Khe'ril Hammerbind.
- [[The Cart of Glittering Paths]] — Tibbin Underbough's traveling jewelry cart in the Court of Air.

## Baldur's Gate
**Type:** City | **Region:** The Sword Coast, northwest of Candlekeep

One of the largest and most powerful cities on the Sword Coast. A sprawling port city divided into the wealthy Upper City, the commercial Lower City, and the rough Outer City district of Rivington. A city of opportunity and corruption in equal measure.

**Party History:**
- Traveled here in Session 2 to investigate the source of the animated books.
- Discovered Amberdune Books in the Wide and uncovered the jackalwere operation.
- Luci stole Nidalia's heart from the jackalweres' house.
- Returned and negotiated with Korvala. Agreed to help resurrect Nidalia.
- The resurrection ritual took place here in Session 3.

**Notable Locations Within:**
- The Wide — the main market district. Where Amberdune Books operated.
- [[Rivington]] (Outer City) — south Baldur's Gate. The Keeper dead drop is here: a broken fountain where Toothsome is meant to deliver the Orcus Figurine.
- [[Nidalia's Network]] — the jackalwere pack (Korvala and the Amberdune Pack) still operate here.

## Beregost
**Type:** Town | **Region:** The Sword Coast, 4-5 hours south of Candlekeep

A modest town on the Trade Way between Candlekeep and Baldur's Gate. More settled and quiet than the great cities but well-traveled by merchants and scholars passing between them. Nidalia the Sphinx has made Beregost her base of operations since her resurrection.

**Party History:**
- The party has not visited Beregost directly yet — but it sits on their route and Nidalia is here.
- Will become a regular stop as the party consults Nidalia between adventures.

**Notable Locations Within:**
- [[Nidalia]] — Sphinx of Lore. The party's oracle and ally. Holds their memories.

## Chalet Brantifax
**Type:** Highland Estate | **Region:** Remote highlands, north of the Green Fields Road

A remote stone chalet built by the late Baron Brantifax on a lonely hilltop, chosen for its seclusion and its proximity to a Shadowfell crossing in the graveyard. After the Baron's death, the Scarlet Sash — a loose order of wereravens — quietly claimed it as a meeting place and safe house. The shadow crossing leads to a Shadowfell necropolis.

**Party History:**
- Current location — Book of the Raven (Session 4 ongoing).
- Party explored the entire chalet in Session 4, splitting into two groups.
- Luci, Toothsome, and Brother Alec explored the basement and graveyard — dug up the Baron's family, found a hex bag, put the poltergeist to rest.
- Morvax and Ash explored the upper floors — found paintings, looted a chest, found the wife's journal.
- Poltergeist of the daughter (Sylphene/Heluthe) was put to rest — she dissolved into yellow light, whispered "thank you for freeing me," and drifted back to her grave.
- Party sent Luci through the black wall in the master bedroom — he found four humans (the wereravens). Session 4 ended here.
- The Orcus Figurine is hidden under rubble in the upper loft — Toothsome plans to take it.

**Notable Locations Within:**
- [[The Scarlet Sash]] — the wereravens using the chalet as a base. Four are present: Madrina Natterask, Taspar Hatchhill, Rennick Groka, Vinique.
