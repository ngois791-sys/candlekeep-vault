> [!important] How This System Works When Ash casts a spell, he rolls a d20. If the roll falls within the current surge threshold, the surge activates. The threshold starts at 1 and grows by 1 each time a spell is cast without triggering. On activation, the threshold resets to 1.
> 
> **Tier Progression:**
> 
> - 5 activations at Tier 1 → promote to Tier 2
> - 3 activations at Tier 2 → promote to Tier 3
> - 1 activation at Tier 3 → full reset back to Tier 1
> 
> **Rolling the Table:** Roll a d100 and halve the result (round up) to get a number between 1 and 50.
