# The Candlekeep Chronicles

*A record kept by order of the Avowed, for the eyes of those who have earned the right to read it.*

---

> [!note] Archivist's Preface
> What follows is a living account of the events surrounding a most unusual company of visitors to our keep. Their arrival on the 2nd day of Tarsakh, in the Year of the Scarlet Witch (1492 DR), was unremarkable. What came after was not.
> 
> These pages are updated as events unfold. The truth, as always, is still being written.

---

The fortress of Candlekeep has stood for over a millennium, its towers rising above the Sword Coast like a beacon for the learned and the lost alike. Its shelves hold more secrets than any single mind could comprehend in a lifetime.

Some of those secrets, it seems, do not wish to remain shelved.
