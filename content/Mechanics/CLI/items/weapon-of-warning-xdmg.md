# Weapon of Warning
*Uncommon (requires attunement)*  
![](Mechanics/CLI/items/img/weapon-of-warning.webp#right)


As long as this weapon is within your reach and you are attuned to it, you and allies within 30 feet of you gain the following benefits.

## Alarm

The weapon magically awakens each subject who is sleeping naturally when combat begins. This benefit doesn't wake a subject from magically induced sleep.

## Supernatural Readiness

Each subject has [Advantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on its [Initiative](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/initiative-xphb.md) rolls.
