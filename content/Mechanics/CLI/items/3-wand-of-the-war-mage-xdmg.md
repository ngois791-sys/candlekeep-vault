# +3 Wand of the War Mage
*Wand, very rare (requires attunement by a spellcaster)*  


- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

While holding this wand, you gain a +3 bonus to spell attack rolls. In addition, you ignore [Half Cover](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/cover-xphb.md) when making a spell attack roll.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 322. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
